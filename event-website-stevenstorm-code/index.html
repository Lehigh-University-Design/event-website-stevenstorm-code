South African Tour

Join Luke Combs for a once-in-a-lifetime musical journey through the stunning landscapes of South Africa on his "Safari Serenade" Tour. Experience the magic of country music set against the backdrop of South Africa's majestic wildlife and breathtaking scenery.

Lainey Wilson

Debut Single: Her debut single "Workin' Overtime" was released in 2018, and it helped to establish her as a rising star in the country music scene. Album: She released her debut studio album, "Sayin' What I'm Thinkin'," in February 2021. The album features a blend of traditional and contemporary country elements. Hit Singles: Some of her popular songs include "Dirty Looks," "Things a Man Oughta Know," and "Sunday Best."

Riley Green

Debut EP: Green released his debut EP, "County Line," in 2018. The EP includes the popular song "There Was This Girl." Debut Album: In 2019, he released his debut studio album, "Different 'Round Here," which further established him as a rising star in the country music scene. Hit Singles: Some of his popular songs include "I Wish Grandpas Never Died," "If It Wasn't for Trucks," "In Love by Now," and "Numbers on the Cars."

Morgan Wallen

Debut EP: Wallen released his debut EP, "Stand Alone," in 2015. Debut Album: In 2018, he released his debut studio album, "If I Know Me," which included the hit single "Up Down." Breakthrough Single: The single "Whiskey Glasses" from his debut album became a massive hit and helped establish him as a prominent figure in the country music industry.

Tour Locations

Cape Town: Cape Town Stadium, nestled against the backdrop of the stunning Table Mountain, provides an unparalleled setting for this music festival. Enjoy breathtaking views of the city while you immerse yourself in the music. Johannesburg: The tour kicks off in the heart of South Africa, Johannesburg, at the Savanna Sunset Arena. Get ready to dance the night away under the African stars. Durban: Feel the rhythm of Luke Combs' country hits on the Zulu Plains, where you can soak up the coastal charm of Durban. Kruger National Park: The grand finale takes place in the world-famous Kruger National Park. Luke will perform on the open-air Safari Stage, surrounded by the sounds of nature and the wild animals of South Africa. A true safari and musical adventure!

Lainey Wilson

Lainey Wilson is known for her traditional country sound mixed with a modern twist. Her music often includes heartfelt and relatable lyrics, drawing on personal experiences.

Riley Green

Riley Green is known for his authentic and down-to-earth country sound. His music often reflects his Southern roots and features relatable, storytelling lyrics.

Morgan Wallen

Morgan Wallen is known for his modern country sound, combining traditional country elements with rock and pop influences. His distinctive gravelly voice sets him apart in the country music scene.
